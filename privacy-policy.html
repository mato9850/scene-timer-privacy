Privacy Policy for Scene Timer

Last Updated: June 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing to play Scene Timer. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app. By downloading and using Scene Timer, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

2. About Scene Timer

Scene Timer is a free, educational and entertaining mobile game designed for adults. It is available on the Amazon Appstore and other platforms. The game may include advertisements and analytics to improve the experience.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, we may automatically collect non-personal data for functionality and analytics purposes. This includes:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • App usage statistics (e.g., session length, screens visited)
  • General location data (e.g., country or region)
  • Anonymous identifiers (e.g., advertising IDs)

4. Third-Party Services

Scene Timer uses third-party services to support advertising and analytics. These third parties may collect and use data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Services we may use include:

  • Amazon Advertising
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase Analytics

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers for more information.

5. How We Use Data

Collected data is used to:

  • Improve game functionality and performance
  • Deliver non-personalized advertisements
  • Analyze user behavior to improve user experience
  • Monitor for bugs and crashes

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Data may be shared with trusted third-party services only for the purposes described above (ads, analytics, crash reporting).

7. Children’s Privacy

Scene Timer is designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect or solicit data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has used the app and provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to secure your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Your Choices

You can manage your privacy settings and ad preferences through your device settings (such as resetting your advertising ID or limiting ad tracking).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last Updated” date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
